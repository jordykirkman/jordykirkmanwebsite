Manifesto

It seems to me that much of society thinks being ambitious and a gamer are mutually exclusive. My life proves they are not. As a member of surely one of the most diverse communities ever, I push for us to be inclusive, accepting and fun loving. Never start a game without a “Good luck”. Never end a game without a “Good game”. We need to understand that other people are there to have fun; not to aide in our entertainment. I think gaming is the social and entertainment platform of the future. But more than that, its a platform for sharing experiences, art and storytelling like nothing else. Its more immersive and interactive than television, film or sports. And contrary to popular stereotypes, I know it is at least as social. Gl hf.
